《死亡诗社》有感

    In the movie ‘Dead Poets Society‘ , Wilton school ,the preparatory school for Ivy League University,was famous for its school motto — Tradition,Honor, Discipline and Excellence, which leads the school to success. However, as time passed, its core content changed. The school turned into a prison and imprison students. And then, Poetry teacher Mr. Keating, used to be a student here. He tried his best to tell his students the right way of life. And strive as much as possible to teach them, protect them against the traditional dogma. I must say that his action affects many students, including Neil Perry, who loves acting. To our disappointment, his father shattered his ‘actor dream’ and takes Neil far away from Mr. Keating, Neil couldn’t understand his father and felt confused, so he committed suicide.

    This is a sad story, but the film is meaningful, “Dead Poets Society” warns us that it is the human’s soul that really matters instead of external force. After all, we should let ourselves return to true, no matter what we face to.
